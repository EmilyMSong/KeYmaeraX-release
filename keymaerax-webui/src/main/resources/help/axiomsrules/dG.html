dG differential ghost adds a new differential equation y′=a(x)*y+b(x) to [{x′=f(x) & Q}]P which, when cleverly chosen, simplifies the analysis of the resulting system after rephrasing P in the resulting [{x′=f(x), y′=a(x)*y+b(x) & Q}]P. This technique is very effective to accumulate facts about the historical evolution of the ODE.
