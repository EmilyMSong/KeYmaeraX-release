dbx Darboux (in)equalities proves ODE invariance for an (in)equality in the postcondition of an ODE system. The user can optionally provide a cofactor term g as tactic input. If g is omitted, the tactic guesses a cofactor automatically.

See also

  • dI Differential invariant to prove the formula R is an invariant of the differential equation system.
