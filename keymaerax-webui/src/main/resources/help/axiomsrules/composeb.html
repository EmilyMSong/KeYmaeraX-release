The composeb axiom splits the safety of a sequential composition a;b, which runs a followed by b, into successive safety properties [a][b]P showing that after all runs of a it is the case that all runs of b are safe, so satisfy P.

Other tactics such as chase and unfold also use this axiom.

  • Use MR monotonicity instead to prove an intermediate condition that always holds after a and is strong enough to imply the safety of b. Using the monotonicity principle can better separate the proof but requires an intermediate condition transporting all required knowledge through a to b.
