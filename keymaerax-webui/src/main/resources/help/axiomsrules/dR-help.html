dR differential refinement replaces the formula the evolution domain Q of a differential equation system [{x'=f(x) & Q}]P with R so that the resulting differential equation system has the form [{x'=f(x) & R}]P.

See also

  • dI Differential invariant to prove the formula R is an invariant of the differential equation system.
  • dC Differential cut: similar to differential refine, but adds R conjunctively to the evolution domain Q rather than replace it.
