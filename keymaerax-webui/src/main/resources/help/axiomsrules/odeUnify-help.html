odeUnify adds the postcondition B of suitable box modality ODE assumptions from the antecedents to the domain constraint of a succedent box modality [{x'=f(x) & Q}]P. The suitability of each assumption is determined by ODE unification that attempts to rewrite the antecedent assumption to match the chosen succedent.
