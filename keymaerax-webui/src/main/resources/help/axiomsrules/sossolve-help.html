sossolve proves a goal in the universal fragment of real arithmetic by finding a sum-of-squares witness. This approach is computationally expensive but yields a highly trustworthy proof of correctness for the arithmetic goal when it succeeds.

See also

  • Tools → QE for quantifier elimination to decide the truth of formulas in real arithmetic.
  • Tools → Counterexample to get potentially faster feedback whether the sequent is suspected true or false.
