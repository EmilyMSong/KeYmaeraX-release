kDomainDiamond replaces postcondition P in ⟨x'=f(x)⟩P to formula R by showing that the ODE x'=f(x) cannot reach R without reaching P first.

See also

  • dV The left premise (diamond modality) of kDomainDiamond with postcondition R can be proved using dV.
  • dI The right premise (box modality) of kDomainDiamond can be proved using ODE proof rules for the box modality.
